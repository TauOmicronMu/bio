Conference Bio

Tom Goodman is an MSci Computer Science w/ Industrial Year student at the University Of Birmingham and the president of the university's Computer Science Society (CSS). He has previously worked at Majestic (majestic.com) as a software developer, and JaGeX Games Studio, Cambridge as a project manager. A passionate developer evangelist, Tom is one of the second cohort of Clarifai Champion, the lead organiser of HackTheMidlands, a committee member on the BCS Birmingham branch and a Silicon Canal ambassador. In his 'free' time he is an avid gamer and enjoys baking - He also loves cats.
